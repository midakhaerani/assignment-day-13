About Us

Haley Model Management was founded in 1977 by Coco Armani. Years of working in this field has taught us a lesson, value not only physical beauty but the nurtured characters of our models. Haley continues to be at the forefront of fashion by establishing the careers of our talents on a holistic level and setting trends within the industry. Haley's belief in the importance of empowering our talents and promoting diversity is driving the industry towards inclusion. Currently, Haley operates offices in Los Angeles and New York.

Our mission, at Haley Model Management, include: embrace diversity, change and inclusiveness; aim to empower the talent we represent so that their voices can be heard, a community that is a safe haven for our talent; supply them with the support to be their true selves.

Haley is not just a company, it is a family.
